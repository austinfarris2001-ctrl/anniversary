What are we drinking?

Tequila, vodka, and a little adventure 🍸

🌅 Tequila Sunrise

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • Grenadine (pour slowly)
  • Don't stir — let it layer

🍋 Paloma

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 4 oz grapefruit juice
  • Lime squeeze + soda
  • Salt rim optional

🍹 Margarita

  • 2 oz tequila
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 1 oz triple sec
  • Shake with ice, salt rim

🍊 Screwdriver

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz orange juice
  • Stir gently over ice
  • Orange slice garnish

🫚 Moscow Mule

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz ginger beer
  • Squeeze of lime
  • Best in a copper mug

🍒 Vodka Cranberry

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz cranberry juice
  • Squeeze of lime
  • Lime wedge garnish
